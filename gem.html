Our Signature Dishes

Murgh Musallam

A royal delicacy featuring tender whole chicken marinated in aromatic spices and slow-cooked to perfection. Rich, flavorful, and beautifully presented, this timeless dish offers an authentic taste of traditional Mughlai cuisine.

Smoky Barbecue Chicken

Juicy chicken infused with a smoky barbecue glaze and grilled to perfection. Packed with bold flavors and a delicious charred aroma, it's a favorite for those who love classic grilled delights.

Spicy Tawa King Prawns

Fresh king prawns tossed on a hot tawa with a blend of handpicked spices, herbs, and seasonings. Every bite delivers a perfect balance of heat, flavor, and succulent seafood goodness.

Arabian Grill Fish

Tender fish marinated in authentic Arabian spices and expertly grilled for a light, flavorful finish. This signature dish combines smoky aromas with delicate flavors for a truly satisfying experience.

Mutton Biryani

Fragrant basmati rice layered with succulent mutton and slow-cooked with traditional spices. Rich in flavor and aroma, this classic biryani is a celebration of authentic culinary heritage.
